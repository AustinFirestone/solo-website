Our Philosophy

Learning is not about memorizing the dots, but rather connecting them. Let computers and AI handle the memorization, note-taking, and holding the dots as much as possible so we can focus on connecting them and thinking creatively at a higher level.

Grasp the idea in person, review the details later

That's why we created Solo.

Smart Bookmarking

Save key moments in your audio recordings by pressing the -Highlight- or -Confused- buttons. Solo will extract the highlights into a list and generate explanations for confusion points.

Custom Quizzes

Turn any lecture into a personalized study tool with custom quizzes for a single note or an entire class.

Quick Review

Review an entire lecture in minutes. Solo will take you through your audio transcript, skipping to your highlights, confusion points, and key concepts determined by the AI.

Instant Flashcards

Automatically generate flashcards from a single note or an entire class. View the flashcards in app or easily export them to Quizlet.

Intelligent Summaries

Go beyond simple summaries with Analogies, Real-World Applications, and Points for Further Research.
